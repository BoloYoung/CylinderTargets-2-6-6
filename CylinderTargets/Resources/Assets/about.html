This sample app illustrates how to use Vuforia to augment objects that are cylindrical in shape – in this case a standard soda can. Upon recognition, the app displays a soccer ball that spins around the can, properly handling occlusion.

To use this app:

  1. Print the target images located at developer.vuforia.com/resources/sample-apps
  2. Cut the target along the indicated lines
  3. Wrap the target around a typical can of soda
  4. Point your device at the wrapped can
  5. When the can is recognized the soccer ball is displayed
